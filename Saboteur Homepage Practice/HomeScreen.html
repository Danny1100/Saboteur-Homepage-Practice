Saboteur except its not shit

Rules

Goal: Either hold the saboteur once the central card pile is exhausted OR be the last player standing

Gameplay: Each player starts with two cards that only they can see. The rest of the cards go into a deck in the middle.
The game starts at one player and goes clockwise around until someone wins.
At the start of each player's turn, they draw a card and can choose to replace one of their cards with the new one. They must then discard a card.
After that, they can (but don't have to) use any card ability even of a card they do not own.
Whenever a player uses a card ability, it can be challenged by any other player. If their ability is challenged, they must show that they own that card.
If they cannot/choose not to prove they have that ability, they discard a card of their choice and must play with one less card for the rest of the game.
However, if they are able to show that they own a card with that ability, the person who challenged permanently loses a card instead.
If a player loses both cards they are eliminated from that game.
If the card that the player loses is a saboteur, it is shuffled back into the deck instead of being discarded.
